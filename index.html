WHY IT STICKS

A stash that beats the bookmark bar you keep ignoring.

Save a tab in one click

Click the vault wheel on any tab and STASH saves the title, favicon, and domain. Hover the logo above if you want the spin.

Tags that filter

Pick design, dev, ai, or finance and STASH narrows the list.

Search the stash

Search titles, domains, notes, and tags as you type.

Ink & Aurora

A dark vault lit by an ember aurora. Light mode when you need it.

Local & private

Your stash lives in local browser storage on your machine. STASH uses no account, sync server, or tracker.

Open source

MIT-licensed and on GitHub. Read it, fork it, ship your own build.
